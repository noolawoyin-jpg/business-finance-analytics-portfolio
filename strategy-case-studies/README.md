# APAC Market Expansion Strategy Case Study

## Overview

This project evaluates APAC expansion opportunities for a fictional B2B SaaS company, **NovaSaaS Inc.**, by comparing **Australia, Singapore, and Japan** as potential first-entry markets.

## Objective

The goal was to identify the strongest market for expansion by analyzing:

* market opportunity
* go-to-market fit
* regulatory environment
* operational complexity
* projected financial upside

## Recommendation

The analysis recommends:

* **Australia** as the best first market for entry
* **Singapore** as a strategic regional hub
* **Japan** as a later-stage expansion opportunity due to higher localization and execution requirements

## What This Project Includes

* executive summary and final recommendation
* market sizing analysis
* weighted market comparison
* financial projections
* go-to-market strategy
* risk assessment and mitigation plan

## Skills Demonstrated

* strategic analysis
* market expansion planning
* financial modeling
* go-to-market strategy
* business decision-making
* executive communication
